How to make the Perfect Bowl of Matcha

Equipment

  • Matcha Powder
  • Chawan (tea bowl)
  • Chasen (bamboo whisk)
  • Chashaku (bamboo scoop)
  • Sifter (optional, for smoother matcha)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sift the matcha powder – removes clumps
  2. Add hot water – around 175°F (not boiling!)
  3. Whisk in an M or W motion – until frothy
  4. Pour into your chawan and admire the froth
  5. Enjoy mindfully – savor the flavor and aroma
