Matcha Latte (Iced)

Prep Time: 5 min | Servings: 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 2 oz hot water
  • 1 cup milk or alt milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Sweetener (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sift matcha into a bowl.
  2. Add hot water and whisk until frothy.
  3. Fill a glass with ice and pour in milk.
  4. Top with matcha mixture. Sweeten to taste!
